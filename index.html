Privacy Policy for Scrapr Cookie Exporter

Effective Date: July 7, 2025
Last Updated: July 7, 2025

Scrapr Cookie Exporter is a Chrome extension that allows users to export their Facebook session cookies into a local cookies.json file for use with the Scrapr automation platform.
